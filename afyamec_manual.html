1. System Access & Security

AfyaMEC protects patient data through strict, multi-layered security protocols.

  • Provider Registration: New accounts must be manually approved by a System Admin before login is permitted[cite: 814, 815].
  • PIN Authentication: Access is secured via a personal 4-to-6 digit numeric PIN[cite: 823, 843].
  • Auto-Logout: To protect unattended devices, the system issues a warning at 25 minutes and automatically logs you out after 30 minutes of inactivity[cite: 18, 19].
  • Offline Mode: If internet is lost, the system securely caches data locally and auto-syncs when reconnected[cite: 20, 21].

3. Clinical Vitals & Triage

Accurate vitals are critical. AfyaMEC automatically interprets readings to protect patient safety.

BMI Calculation

Enter Weight and Height; the system auto-calculates BMI. A BMI ≥ 30 triggers an Obesity warning, mapping to MEC Category 3 for certain methods[cite: 1906, 1910].

BP Categorization

Blood pressure is instantly categorized from "Normal" to "Severe HTN"[cite: 1787]. High readings automatically flag cardiovascular conditions for the MEC screener[cite: 1888].


🚨 Crisis Alerts

If BP is ≥180/110, a hard stop alert fires: "Hypertensive Crisis Detected. Do NOT initiate hormonal contraceptives. Refer for emergency management." [cite: 1888, 1889]

4. WHO Pregnancy Exclusion

Before issuing any method, pregnancy must be reasonably ruled out[cite: 1983, 1984].

  • PDT Entry: Record the result of a physical pregnancy test[cite: 1973, 1977].
  • WHO 6-Question Checklist: If no PDT is available, ask the 6 standard WHO questions (e.g., LAM criteria, abstinence, recent delivery)[cite: 1967, 1968, 1969].
  • Automated Logic: A "YES" to any of the 6 questions safely rules out pregnancy to proceed[cite: 1993, 1994].

⚠️ Pregnancy Confirmed

If a positive PDT is recorded, the system halts the family planning session and advises immediate referral to Antenatal Care (ANC)[cite: 1981, 1982].

5. Medical Eligibility (MEC) Screening

The screener asks targeted questions to build a comprehensive health profile[cite: 1927, 1931, 1934].

  • Reproductive History: Breastfeeding timing, past ectopics, active PID/STIs[cite: 1927, 1929].
  • Neurological: Differentiates between migraines with and without aura, checking patient age[cite: 1937, 1948].
  • ARV Interactions: Specifically checks for Rifampicin and Efavirenz-based regimens, which reduce hormonal efficacy[cite: 1942, 1943].

Smart Detection

Any condition flagged "YES" automatically impacts the next stage. The system combines these answers with the auto-detected Vitals (BMI/BP) to generate a complete MEC profile[cite: 1944, 1945].

6. Method Choice & The MEC Wheel

The system evaluates the client's conditions against WHO MEC guidelines to filter safe methods[cite: 1727, 1728].

  • Category 1 & 2: Recommended and generally safe[cite: 1710, 1711].
  • Category 3: Use with caution; risks usually outweigh benefits[cite: 1710, 1711].
  • Category 4: Contraindicated. The system strictly blocks selection of these methods[cite: 1710, 1711, 1713].
  • Interactive Wheel: Use the digital MEC Wheel tool to manually explore interactions and teach clients[cite: 917, 918].

7. Counselling & Provision (BCS+)

Record the exact clinical actions taken during provision:

  • DISC DMPA-SC Tracking: Log whether the injection is Provider-Administered (PA) or Self-Injected (SI)[cite: 1619, 1620].
  • Take-Home Doses: Record SI training status and dispense up to 3 take-home vials[cite: 1634, 1635].
  • STI/Cervical Screening: Log HIV testing, IPV screening, Cervical Cancer screening methods (VIA/PAP), and condom provision[cite: 1659, 1666, 1668].

Method Switch Guide

If a client is changing methods, the built-in library provides step-by-step guidance (e.g., switching from COC to Implant seamlessly without losing protection)[cite: 966].

8. Automated Reporting & DHIS2

AfyaMEC eliminates manual tallying by automatically compiling your session data into official Ministry of Health formats.

MOH 512

The daily Family Planning Client Register. View pagination online or export the complete log to Excel[cite: 1045, 1083].

MOH 711 & DHIS2

Generates the Section D (FP) and Section G (Cervical Cancer) monthly summary. Push data directly to KHIS via the DHIS2 integration[cite: 596, 1157, 1186].

MOH 747A & DISC

Manage commodity stock levels (FCDRR) and push specialized DMPA-SC self-injection metrics directly to ONA Enketo[cite: 664, 1308].
